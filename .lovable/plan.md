
# Saiju M — UI/UX Designer Portfolio

A modern, professional single-page portfolio website with smooth scroll navigation and clean aesthetics.

## Design Direction
- Clean, modern look with a professional color palette (dark navy/charcoal accents on white)
- Smooth scroll-linked animations as sections come into view
- Sticky top navigation bar with smooth scroll to sections
- Professional typography with good hierarchy

- Brief intro based on the objective
